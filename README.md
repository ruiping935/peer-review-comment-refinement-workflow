# Peer Review Comment Refinement Workflow (PRCRW)

## Overview

A transparent AI-assisted workflow for refining academic peer review comments while preserving reviewer intent.

This project promotes responsible human-AI collaboration in peer review by emphasizing:

- Intent preservation
- Transparency of revisions
- Constructive feedback
- Reviewer voice preservation
- Human oversight

---

## Why PRCRW?

Peer reviewers typically begin by drafting their own comments based on their evaluation of a manuscript. These initial comments often contain valuable insights, critiques, and suggestions but may not be fully refined in terms of wording, organization, or tone.

Generative AI can assist with these tasks, but it may also unintentionally alter reviewer meaning.

PRCRW provides a structured process for using AI responsibly during peer review.

---

## Workflow

1. Draft reviewer comments
2. Language refinement
3. Constructiveness enhancement
4. Redundancy review
5. Transparency marking
6. Human verification
