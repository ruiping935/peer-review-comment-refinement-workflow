# PRCRW v1.0 Prompt

Apply the Peer Review Comment Refinement Workflow (PRCRW) v1.0.

I am the reviewer. Please refine my reviewer comments according to the following rules.

## Goals

* Preserve reviewer intent.
* Improve grammar, clarity, and readability.
* Maintain a constructive, professional, and respectful tone.
* Avoid AI-sounding language.
* Maintain my writing style as much as possible.
* Keep comments concise.

## Requirements

### Intent Preservation

* Do not change the substantive meaning of my comments.
* Do not introduce new criticisms that I did not raise.
* Do not remove concerns that I raised.

### Quoted Manuscript Text

* If I quote text from the manuscript, preserve the quoted text exactly as written.
* Only correct obvious transcription errors that I introduced while typing.

### Constructive Feedback

* If I identify a concern but do not explain how the authors could improve it, provide a practical suggestion or guiding question when appropriate.
* Suggestions should remain aligned with my original concern.

### Reviewer Voice

* Maintain a clear, direct, professional, and respectful tone.
* Avoid overly formal or AI-sounding language.
* Preserve my writing style whenever possible.

### Transparency

* Bold every AI-generated modification, addition, clarification, grammar correction, or wording change relative to my original comments.
* Do not bold text that already appeared in my original comments.
* If I quoted manuscript text, do not bold it unless correcting a transcription error.

### Redundancy

* Do not automatically merge comments.
* If comments appear to overlap, identify potential redundancy separately.

### Length

* Keep comments concise.
* A typical journal review should remain approximately 1–2 pages when appropriate.

## Output Format

Provide:

### Refined Reviewer Comments

A complete refined version of the reviewer comments with all AI-generated edits marked in bold.

### Potential Overlap or Redundancy

List comments that may address similar concerns without combining them.
