# Peer Review Comment Refinement Workflow (PRCRW)

Version 1.0

Developed by Ruiping Huang

---

## Purpose

The Peer Review Comment Refinement Workflow (PRCRW) is a structured framework designed to improve the clarity, professionalism, and constructiveness of academic peer review comments while preserving reviewer intent.

The workflow supports responsible human-AI collaboration by emphasizing transparency, reviewer voice preservation, and human oversight.

---

## Scope

PRCRW can be used for:

* Journal manuscript reviews
* Conference paper reviews

PRCRW is intended to improve communication quality and should not alter the substantive meaning of reviewer comments.

---

## Core Principles

### Principle 1: Intent Preservation

The original meaning of reviewer comments should remain unchanged.

AI may improve wording and organization but should not introduce new criticisms or remove existing concerns.

### Principle 2: Transparency

All AI-generated revisions should be identifiable.

Reviewers should be able to distinguish between original content and AI-generated modifications.

### Principle 3: Respect for Source Text

Quoted text from the manuscript should remain unchanged except for obvious transcription errors introduced during note taking.

### Principle 4: Constructive Feedback

Whenever possible, reviewer comments should include actionable suggestions that help authors improve their work.

### Principle 5: Reviewer Voice Preservation

The final review should reflect the reviewer's preferred writing style rather than the default style of an AI system.

---

## Workflow

### Step 1. Draft Reviewer Comments

The reviewer writes initial comments.

Focus:

* Content
* Concerns
* Initial impressions

Grammar and style are not the primary focus at this stage.

Output:

* Raw reviewer comments

---

### Step 2. Language Refinement

Revise:

* Grammar
* Spelling
* Punctuation
* Readability

Requirements:

* Preserve meaning
* Avoid AI-sounding language
* Maintain reviewer voice

Output:

* Refined reviewer comments

---

### Step 3. Constructiveness Enhancement

Identify opportunities to provide:

* Actionable suggestions
* Clarifying questions
* Requests for additional evidence
* Reporting recommendations

Output:

* Constructive reviewer comments

---

### Step 4. Redundancy Review

Identify:

* Repeated comments
* Overlapping concerns
* Opportunities for consolidation

Output:

* Redundancy report

---

### Step 5. Transparency Marking

Mark all AI-generated revisions.

Possible approaches:

* Bold formatting
* Track changes
* Revision logs

Output:

* Revision-tracked comments

---

### Step 6. Human Verification

The reviewer evaluates:

* Meaning preservation
* Accuracy
* Tone
* Completeness

The reviewer retains final authority over all revisions.

Output:

* Final review report

---

## Deliverables

PRCRW produces:

1. Polished reviewer comments
2. Revision-tracked reviewer comments
3. Redundancy report

---

## Human Oversight Checklist

Before submission, verify:

* No change in substantive meaning
* No incorrect interpretation
* No unsupported criticism
* No loss of reviewer voice
* No accidental modification of quoted manuscript text
